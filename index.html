Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

WiseBook ("WiseBook", "we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile learning app that helps users practice and remember books and topics using AI-generated questions, spaced repetition, and progress tracking.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate and improve WiseBook.

  • Account information: If you sign in with Google, we may collect your name and email address.
  • Anonymous account data: If you use WiseBook without signing in, we assign an anonymous account identifier so the app can function.
  • Learning data: We collect information related to your use of the app, including books or topics you enter, generated flashcards, answers, scores, streaks, progress, study activity, and session performance.
  • Derived data: We derive learning metrics such as accuracy, mastery, book level, streaks, and other progress indicators from your activity.
  • Device-related data: We may store a device identifier and detect your device locale or language settings to support sync, analytics, and region-appropriate content. We do not collect GPS or precise location data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate WiseBook.
  • Generate personalized questions and flashcards.
  • Track learning progress and spaced-repetition schedules.
  • Sync progress across devices when you sign in.
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and analytics.
  • Respond to user support or privacy requests.

3. AI Processing

WiseBook uses third-party AI services to generate question content based on the books or topics you enter. We store the resulting study content and progress data needed to provide the service.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate WiseBook, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and backend infrastructure.
  • Google Sign-In when you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Anthropic Claude or similar AI providers for question generation.

These providers process data on our behalf to support the app.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain your progress, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

6. Your Choices and Rights

  • You may use WiseBook anonymously without signing in.
  • You may delete your account and associated data from the Profile screen in the app.
  • You may contact us to request help with privacy-related questions.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

WiseBook is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. International Users

If you use WiseBook from outside India, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: rakesh@wisebook.ai
